## 什么是热封培养杯？

热封培养杯（Heat-Sealing Culture Cup）是一种无盖组织培养容器。

与传统组培瓶通过瓶盖密封不同，热封培养杯在接种完成后采用专用热封膜进行封口，并通过自动热封设备完成密封，从而形成稳定的无菌培养环境。

富龙塑业热封培养杯采用PP（聚丙烯）材质制造，具有重量轻、成本低、适合批量生产等特点。

## 热封膜上的黑色定位点有什么作用？

许多客户第一次接触热封培养体系时都会注意到热封膜上的黑色定位点。

这些定位点并不是装饰，而是自动热封设备的识别标记。

热封设备通过识别定位点实现：

* 自动送料
* 自动定位
* 自动热封

其工作原理与食品包装设备上的定位标记类似。

这种自动化封口方式能够显著提高生产效率，特别适合商业化组织培养工厂的大批量生产。

## 为什么商业化组培工厂选择热封培养杯？

## 1. 成本更低

热封培养杯结构简单，无需配套瓶盖。

相比传统带盖培养容器，整体使用成本更低，特别适合培养数量较大的项目。

## 2. 适合规模化生产

热封培养体系可以实现：

* 批量装杯
* 批量接种
* 批量封口

特别适用于商业化微繁殖（Commercial Micropropagation）生产。

## 3. 支持辐照灭菌

热封培养杯和热封膜均可采用辐照灭菌处理。

使用前即可保持无菌状态，减少灭菌准备工作并降低污染风险。

## 4. 方形结构提高空间利用率

相比圆形容器，方形培养杯能够：

* 提高培养架利用率
* 提高运输效率
* 提高仓储效率

对于大型组培基地具有明显优势。

## 5. 用后处理简单

许多商业化项目更关注生产效率而非重复使用。

热封培养杯使用完成后无需回收和清洗，可减少人工投入并简化生产流程。

## 哪些植物组织培养项目经常使用热封培养杯？

热封培养杯广泛应用于：

* 马铃薯脱毒快繁
* 石斛组织培养
* 兰花组织培养
* 商品化种苗生产
* 商业化微繁殖生产

在培养数量较大的生产型组培基地中应用尤为普遍。

## 热封培养杯和组培瓶应该如何选择？

组培瓶更适合：

* 长期培养
* 可重复使用
* 科研实验
* 高价值种苗培养

热封培养杯更适合：

* 成本控制
* 批量生产
* 自动化封口
* 商业化繁育

两者并不存在替代关系，而是适用于不同的组织培养体系。

## 结语

对于追求高效率和低成本的商业化组织培养项目而言，热封培养杯已经成为马铃薯、石斛、兰花等植物组织培养的重要容器选择。通过配套热封膜和自动热封设备，热封培养体系能够实现稳定、高效和规模化的种苗生产。

## What Is a Heat-Sealing Culture Cup?

A Heat-Sealing Culture Cup is a lid-free tissue culture container designed for commercial propagation systems.

Instead of using a conventional culture lid, the cup is sealed with a specialized heat-sealing film after inoculation. An automated sealing machine then creates a secure seal, maintaining a stable and sterile culture environment.

Fulong Plastic heat-sealing culture cups are manufactured from polypropylene (PP), offering lightweight construction, low cost, and suitability for large-scale production.

## What Are the Black Marks on the Sealing Film?

Many customers notice small black marks printed on heat-sealing films.

These marks are not decorative. They serve as positioning marks for automated sealing machines.

By detecting these marks, the machine can perform:

* Automatic film feeding
* Automatic positioning
* Automatic heat sealing

This automated process significantly improves production efficiency and consistency, especially in commercial tissue culture laboratories and propagation facilities.

## Why Do Commercial Tissue Culture Facilities Choose Heat-Sealing Culture Cups?

## 1. Lower Production Cost

Heat-sealing culture cups have a simple structure and do not require separate culture lids.

Compared with traditional culture vessels, they provide a more cost-effective solution for large-volume production.

## 2. Suitable for Large-Scale Production

The heat-sealing system supports:

* Batch filling
* Batch inoculation
* Batch sealing

This makes it particularly suitable for commercial micropropagation operations.

## 3. Compatible with Radiation Sterilization

Both the culture cups and sealing films can be sterilized using gamma irradiation.

Pre-sterilized packaging helps reduce preparation time and lowers contamination risks.

## 4. Square Design Improves Space Efficiency

Compared with round containers, square culture cups can:

* Increase shelf utilization
* Improve transportation efficiency
* Optimize storage capacity

These advantages become increasingly important in large-scale propagation facilities.

## 5. Easy Post-Production Handling

Many commercial projects prioritize production efficiency over container reuse.

After use, heat-sealing culture cups can be discarded without washing or recovery procedures, helping reduce labor requirements.

## Common Applications of Heat-Sealing Culture Cups

Heat-sealing culture cups are widely used for:

* Potato tissue culture and virus-free propagation
* Dendrobium tissue culture
* Orchid micropropagation
* Commercial seedling production
* Large-scale plant micropropagation

They are especially popular in facilities handling high production volumes.

## Heat-Sealing Culture Cups vs Traditional Culture Vessels

## Traditional Culture Vessels Are Better For:

* Long-term culture
* Repeated use
* Laboratory research
* High-value plant materials

## Heat-Sealing Culture Cups Are Better For:

* Cost control
* Mass production
* Automated sealing systems
* Commercial propagation

Rather than replacing each other, these two systems serve different production needs within the tissue culture industry.

## Conclusion

For commercial tissue culture projects focused on efficiency, consistency, and cost control, heat-sealing culture cups have become an important cultivation solution.

Combined with pre-cut sealing films and automated heat-sealing equipment, they enable reliable, scalable, and highly efficient production of potato, dendrobium, orchid, and other tissue culture plants.
